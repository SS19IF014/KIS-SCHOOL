Primary School

The Primary school takes pride in the incomparable learning style and unique method of facilitating understanding in the classroom. The school's approach to the ICSE curriculum is child centric and thought provoking. It encompasses a conglomerate of diverse learning methodologies involving project work, audio-visual aids, hands on activities and inquiry based learning. Hence, by rendering opportunities for experiential learning, the dormant potential of the child is stimulated, further developing the intellect of the child .

An environment conducive to learning is created by a well qualified and equipped teaching faculty. The teachers invest their time in organising and implementing effective teaching practices to enhance student learning.

At Kohinoor International School, learning is continuous and progressive, culminating in the creation of able and audacious young citizens.

Middle School

The Middle School curriculum formulated by the Indian Council of Secondary Education (ICSE) programme lays a great emphasis on learning basic facts and gradually move to detailed understanding of concepts. It ensures progression and continuity across various stages of learning, incorporates continuous evaluation, review and remediation of children’s performance. It aims to develop skills related to writing, reading, reflecting, critical thinking, public speaking, fostering initiative among students and creating a desire to acquire knowledge. Academic subjects include English, Hindi, Marathi, Mathematics, Science, History, Geography and Computer Science. Art, Communication Skills, Physical Education, Socially Useful Productive Work (SUPW), Music and Martial Arts are integrated into the mainstream curriculum. Children are encouraged to foster team- building and competitive spirit through projects, activities, research and presentations which are linked to learning experiences. Remedial teaching is done in class to help the children to understand the concepts better and to work with understanding. Assessments are summative along with orals or assignments (child-centric approach).We have incorporated Detailed Assessments for English, Mathematics and Science which is executed by ‘Educational Initiatives’ an institute that focuses on personalized learning with understanding. It is a customized assessment programme which presents immediate detailed feedback and enhances critical thinking and also caters to remedial teaching to make the concepts clearer and have a stronger base. The Middle School curriculum is built around the principle of knowledge, understanding, application and skill-sets.

High School

The secondary section namely grades 9 and 10 of Kohinoor International School follows the curriculum designed by the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination , a private, non-governmental board of school education in India.
The subjects that are offered at KIS are divided into three groups :

Group I
includes Compulsory Subjects - English, History & Civics, & Geography, Hindi and Socially Useful and Productive Work (Graded Subject)

Group II
which includes a choice between
Mathematics OR Commercial Studies & Science (Physics, Biology, Chemistry) OR Economics

Group III
It has any one from - Computer Applications, Art, Home Science, Physical Education
All subjects have components of internal assessment that are carried out on the basis of assignments/project work, practicals and coursework. There is 20% weightage for internal assessment in group I and II and 50% weightage for group III.
Despite the secondary being the board classes, we at KIS make the teaching-learning process stress free with a lot of interesting activities which are integrated into the curriculum with equal emphasis on sports,music, competitions, educational tours and excursions.
Our continuous assessment pattern helps the students to be better prepared for the board examination.
Our ex - students vouch that their alma mater has not only made them global citizens but has also helped them to face life with its many challenges.
